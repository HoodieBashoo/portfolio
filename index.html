Overview

Game Developer and 3D artist inspired by sources such as the layered stories of Xenoblade, the tight mechanics of Bloodborne, and the dungeons/systems of King's Field and Daggerfall. With about 4 years of experience under my belt, I strive to create games that inspire others in the same way.

I aim for a deeper understanding of all systems relating to games, and a huge goal of mine is to create my own engine someday. In the meantime, I am working on a full-scale project doubling as a programming testground to experiment in and gain as much experience as possible.
